Assignment 9: Mapbox

Make a Mapbox style

  1. Create a Mapbox account if you do not have one.
  2. Starting with an existing style, customize the style.
  3. Upload your own data as a tileset and style it.
  4. Publish the style.
  5. Use the style in a Carto map.
  6. Publish your map online with Github pages.

Work on your final project

When you proposed your project you included a milestone that you would be able to finish by April 19th. Work on your project and aim to meet that milestone.

Submitting your work

Submit a link to your work.

Please include a short paragraph response or summary of your work. Indicate how you changed the styling and which data you uploaded.
